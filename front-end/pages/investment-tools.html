Essential Investment Tools

Comprehensive collection of tools to empower your investment journey

Research & Analysis

Find and evaluate potential investments with these essential tools:

  • Stock Screeners: Finviz, Yahoo Finance
  • Financial News: Bloomberg, Reuters
  • Fundamental Analysis: Morningstar, SEC EDGAR
  • Technical Analysis: TradingView, Thinkorswim
Trading Platforms

Platforms where you actually place buy and sell orders:

  • Online Brokerages: Fidelity, Vanguard, Charles Schwab
  • Robo-Advisors: Betterment, Wealthfront, SoFi Invest
  • Advanced Platforms: Interactive Brokers, E*TRADE
Portfolio Management

Monitor your overall investment performance:

  • Portfolio Trackers: Personal Capital, Mint
  • Performance Calculators: Bankrate Calculator
  • Tax Optimization: TurboTax, H&R Block
  • Asset Allocation: Portfolio Visualizer
Educational Resources

Build knowledge and plan your strategy:

  • Compound Interest: SEC Calculator
  • Retirement Planners: Fidelity Planner
  • Paper Trading: Investopedia Simulator
  • Investment Courses: Khan Academy, Coursera

Key Insight

Investment tools are meant to empower your decision-making, not replace it. They provide data and efficiency, but the strategy and discipline still come from you.
